Before writing your CV and searching a job the first question you need to ask yourself is what job role do you want?

Within engineering there are multiple different job roles and there are also many roles that aren't engineering but still in tech that may be a good fit if you like creating tech products but don't want a coding heavy role.

Below is a summary of different roles that you may want to look at with a brief description of each. It is important to know that these are not the only roles, and each role may differ at each company so it's important to read the job description thoroughly and ask during the interview process any questions you may have as to how the role works in practice.
