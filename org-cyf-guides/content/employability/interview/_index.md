There are many different types of interviews you may encounter whilst applying for jobs in tech.

They may be virtual or in person, and often a combination. You may have some initial stages that are virtual, and then a final stage in person. For a more comprehensive guide to each of the interview types please see the guides below.

For some example questions, the following have been recommended by our trainees. See [this resource for technical questions](https://github.com/DopplerHQ/awesome-interview-questions?tab=readme-ov-file#windows) and [this resource for behavioural questions](https://github.com/ashishps1/awesome-behavioral-interviews?tab=readme-ov-file).
